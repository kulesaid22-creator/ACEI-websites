Albertine Community Empowerment Initiative (ACEI)

Empowering communities around Lake Albert through education, skills & women/youth empowerment.

About ACEI

ACEI is a community-focused organisation working in the Lake Albert region of Uganda to improve access to education, provide vocational training, and support women and youth through entrepreneurship and savings groups.

Programs

  • Education Support: School materials, mentoring and retention support for children.
  • Vocational Training: Tailoring, carpentry, ICT basics and agribusiness skills for youth.
  • Women Empowerment: Savings groups, financial literacy and micro-enterprise support.
  • Community Outreach: Health, sanitation and environmental awareness workshops.

Expected Impact

  • Higher school attendance and retention.
  • Income-generating activities established by women groups.
  • Skilled youth with practical vocational skills.
  • Stronger local leadership and community participation.

Get Involved

We welcome volunteers, partners and donors. There are several ways to help:

  1. Volunteer as a trainer or mentor.
  2. Donate materials or funds to support activities.
  3. Partner with us on community projects.
